Career

Over the years with Progress Rail, I have held several roles:

I began as an account executive, and quickly progressed to the role of class one account executive where I utilized analytical skills to develop and implement reporting tools. My success in simplifying day-to-day operations garnered attention from the IT department, who later approached me to assist with companywide reporting requests.

Within IT, I gained valuable exposure to departments globally within the organization, including Finance, HR, Quality, Safety, Procurement, Warehousing, Production, and Sales. I also gained significant experience working with various enterprise systems such as D365, SAP, and Infor LN.

Today, I serve as team lead for Power BI report development. In this capacity, I am responsible for managing the team's workflow and ensuring that requests are completed to full specifications. Additionally, I am part responsible for managing the enterprise's master data, ensuring quality and accuracy of our data across multiple systems.
